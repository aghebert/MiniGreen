Tl;dr – 
Users – has the user id, name, and password
Category is good for storing what products go where
Alerts is what is stored after the program goes through the list and sees what is needed most
Products is the ‘header’ of any number of individual products. IE: it says spoons, but no details
ItemCollections is the details of each item, which is connected to products. It will have the most info, and the most detailed info.
Didn’t include Supplier, as I don’t think it’s necessary at this basic level
